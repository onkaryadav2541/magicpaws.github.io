About Us

We are a nonprofit animal welfare organisation working to create a safe and healthy world for every animal, and for the people who love them.AMTM is here for every animal in need, and has been since 2010. We believe that every animal deserves the best. That’s why we are dedicated to the rescue and rehabilitation of stray and abandoned animals — we believe we can transform their quality of life through kindness and compassion.
